# Crypto News Digest — Specification

## Goal
Generate a daily crypto news digest that filters genuine market-moving signals from hype, FUD, and noise — giving readers a clear, honest picture of what actually matters today.

## Target Audience
Crypto investors and enthusiasts who are overwhelmed by the 24/7 news cycle and want a concise, no-BS summary they can read in 5 minutes.

## Requirements
1. Cover the top 5-8 stories from the past 24 hours, ranked by actual market impact
2. Each story must include: headline, 2-3 sentence summary, source credibility rating (1-5), and a "signal vs noise" verdict
3. Flag any stories that are recycled narratives, paid promotions, or unverified rumors
4. Include a "Market Pulse" section with BTC/ETH price context and overall sentiment
5. Track developing stories across multiple days with a "Continuing Saga" tag
6. Provide a "What This Actually Means" plain-English interpretation for each major story
7. Separate on-chain data and verifiable facts from speculation and opinion
8. Include a "Watch List" of 2-3 emerging stories that haven't gone mainstream yet
9. Note any regulatory developments with jurisdiction and potential timeline

## Output Format
Structured digest with: Market Pulse header → Top Stories (ranked) → BS Alert section → Watch List → 1-line "TL;DR of the Day"

## Quality Criteria
- Zero tolerance for amplifying unverified claims without flagging them
- Price references include timeframe and percentage moves, not just "pumped" or "dumped"
- Sources cited for every factual claim
- Clear distinction between news and editorial opinion
- Accessible to intermediate crypto users without dumbing down for experts
