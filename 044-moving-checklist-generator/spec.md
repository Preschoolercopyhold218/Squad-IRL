# Moving Checklist Generator — Specification

## Goal
Generate a comprehensive, timeline-based moving checklist customized to the move type (local/long-distance/international), living situation, and household size — so nothing falls through the cracks during one of life's most stressful events.

## Target Audience
Anyone planning a move: renters, homeowners, families, solo movers — from across-town apartments to cross-country relocations.

## Requirements
1. Generate a week-by-week countdown timeline starting 8 weeks before move day
2. Organize tasks by category: administrative, packing, utilities, logistics, financial, cleaning, personal
3. Include specific deadlines for time-sensitive items (lease notice periods, mail forwarding, utility transfers)
4. Provide a room-by-room packing strategy with priority order
5. Include a "moving day" hour-by-hour schedule from wake-up to first night in new place
6. List commonly forgotten tasks: prescriptions transfer, voter registration, pet records, gym cancellations
7. Provide cost estimates for major expenses (movers, supplies, deposits, cleaning)
8. Include a "first open" essentials box list for surviving the first 48 hours
9. Add decision checkpoints: DIY vs movers, what to sell/donate/trash
10. Note tasks that differ based on renting vs owning (security deposit photos, final walkthrough)

## Output Format
8-week countdown timeline → Room-by-room packing guide → Moving day schedule → Essentials box list → Budget worksheet → Post-move settling checklist

## Quality Criteria
- Deadlines are realistic and account for lead times (2 weeks for mail forwarding, etc.)
- Tasks are specific and actionable, not vague ("call Comcast to transfer service" not "handle internet")
- Nothing important is missing — the "oh no, I forgot" moments are eliminated
- Budget estimates are in realistic ranges, not arbitrary numbers
- Customized to the move type, not a generic one-size-fits-all list
